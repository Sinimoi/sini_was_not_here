Singer / Kotisirkka


Galleria Alli 2019 & Taiteen koititalo 2020
Kerava


The first version of the work was a noisy sculpture called
Singer. It had a circular saw wheel attached to
a washing machine drum with steel strings
which made it "sing", when the saw was turned around.
The next version called Kotisirkka — a Finnish name for
a house cricket, was without noise or movement.
The cricket got wings, eyes and "laid eggs".
From Kerava it went to
"Haihatus" 2021 Summer exhibition in Joutsa.
