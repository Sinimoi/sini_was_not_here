Linda

2008-2009

The first Linda was made in collaboration
of five people in a Dance and media art -workshop.

Linda appeared eventually in three different occasions and with varying crews.
During the performances Linda moves, makes sounds and gets somehow out of control.
Linda III used the wide corridor of a new shopping centre in Rovaniemi,
even though her new red shoes slided on the slippery floor.
