Karmenaattori

2013

Duration 10-15min

Karmenaattori is a work inspired by Georges Bizet's opera Carmen,
and human's desire to shape bodies technologically.
Karmenaattori was the first part of my
MA graduation work in University of The Arts Helsinki.
It was shown 12 times in the Graduation show in 2013.
I also showed Karmenaattori in 2015
in a performance festival Performanssifiesta in Tampere.
