Karmenaattori

2013

Duration 10-15min

Karmenaattori is a show inspired by the Carmen opera by Georges Bizet,
and by human's desire to shape bodies technologically.

In the beginning the curtain drops rattling. It reveals The Karmenaattori, lying on her back, then winding herself up onto her long steel legs
with a winch attached on her belly.
When she starts walking,The mechanisms on her legs
start rotating, and the noise being produced is amplified
through a megafon attached on her head.
In the end the Karmenaattori strips off the safe but restricting harness
and catches a long crutch to stop from falling.

Karmenaattori was the first part of my MA graduation work in University of The Arts Helsinki.
It was shown 12 times in the Graduation show in 2013.
I also showed Karmenaattori in 2015 in a performance festival Performanssifiesta in Tampere.
